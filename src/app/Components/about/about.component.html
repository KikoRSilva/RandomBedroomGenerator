About

This website was built based on people's needs. By any chance, during your vacation with family or friends, have you ever had problems choosing the beds or rooms? So, I decided to solve this problem with this random room generator in order to make the allocation of rooms fair.

I hope that this site will help in the dilemma of choosing rooms or beds. To be completely fair, assign a number to a room or bed and, depending on the result of the generator, it is automatically assigned.

None of the information entered on this website will be stored or used for other purposes only to generate random results.

If you found any problems or want to contact me, please email me at support@fsdev.com
